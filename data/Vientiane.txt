Vientiane is the capital and largest city of Laos, located on the banks of the Mekong River. The city is known for its laid-back atmosphere, Buddhist temples, and French colonial architecture. Key landmarks in Vientiane include the Pha That Luang stupa, the Patuxai monument, and the Wat Si Saket temple. Vientiane is also a hub for Lao culture and a gateway to exploring the natural beauty of Laos.
