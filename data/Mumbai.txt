Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, is the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra and the financial, commercial, and entertainment capital of India. Located on the west coast of India, Mumbai is known for its bustling port, colonial architecture, and vibrant culture. The city is famous for being home to Bollywood, the largest film industry in the world, as well as landmarks like the Gateway of India, Marine Drive, and the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus. Mumbai is also renowned for its street food, lively markets, and as a major hub for business, finance, and technology.
