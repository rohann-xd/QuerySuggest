Washington, D.C., is the capital of the United States, located on the Potomac River. Known for its historical landmarks, Washington, D.C. is home to iconic monuments such as the Lincoln Memorial, the Washington Monument, and the U.S. Capitol Building. The city is also the political heart of the nation, housing government institutions like the White House and the Supreme Court. Additionally, it offers a wealth of museums and cultural institutions, including those on the National Mall.
