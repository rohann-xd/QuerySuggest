Tokyo is the capital and largest city of Japan, known for its futuristic skyline, ancient temples, and cutting-edge technology. The city is a global center for finance, technology, and fashion, and is famous for landmarks such as the Tokyo Tower, Senso-ji Temple, and Shibuya Crossing. Tokyo is also known for its vibrant neighborhoods, such as Harajuku and Akihabara, and its incredible food scene, including sushi, ramen, and tempura.
